About Me

Hello, My name is Milo and I am a 2.5 year old English Bulldog! I was born and raised in Atwater, CA before moving to San Francisco, CA at the age of 6 months old to live with my dad (and now my mom too!).

I am also a certified therapy dog for Jewish Family and Children's Services. In my career as a therapy dog I have visited a couple of retirement homes and have met some really wonderful people.
